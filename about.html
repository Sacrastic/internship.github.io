About Us

A child truly learns by coming in contact with the subject matter and not by merely listening or reading about it. One cannot imbibe or experience knowledge by sitting within four walls. That is why it is essential to take children out of the classrooms into an open environment which is conducive to experiencing first hand of the lessons a child must learn.
